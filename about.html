About Us

Schokobanana Studios is an independent game development studio driven by a passion for creating memorable gaming experiences. We enjoy experimenting with new ideas, building unique worlds, and sharing our projects with players around the world.

From small prototypes to full releases, every project is an opportunity to learn something new and improve our craft. Whether it's programming, art, music, or storytelling, we strive to create games that are fun, polished, and full of personality.
