Tribes of Areus

In Development

Tribes of Areus is a mobile strategy and building game set in the stone age. Become a cheftain and expand you tribe. Explore the vast lands of Areus. Gather resources, master new crafts, recruit other cheftains and hunt or tame wild creatures to build and strengthen your tribe. Uncover the secrets of a world brimming with danger and wonder.

Skull Knight

Released

Skull Knight is a 2D medieval pixel art game, where the player takes on the role of Sir Richard Archibald, a young knight who must battle hordes of monsters and face powerful bosses. Collect gear, learn awesome fighting combos and experience an amazing story as you follow legendary characters on their journey!
